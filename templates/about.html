About the project!

Why goldfish?

Goldfish Guard is a password manager with the memeory of a goldfish. But what does that mean? Well, after you type in your information, it is immediately encrypted using a unique key that only you have. The encrypted data is then stored, and can only be decrypted using the key! At no point in the process will your unencrypted password be stored, hence the 'memory of the goldfish.'
